Selected Impact

Remoti

Identified through funnel analysis that 75% of candidates were dropping at the assessment stage, and redesigned the process sequence — cutting time-to-hire from 45 to 20 days. Then built a rule-based pipeline system in Google Apps Script that raised the weekly throughput of the 4-recruiter team I led from 30 to 50 candidates.

Full Stack Labs

Replaced a 2-week shadowing onboarding with a structured knowledge base built in Notion and Loom, adopted company-wide to standardize how the full recruiting team ramped up and operated — while leading a pod of two recruiters and a sourcer.

Blackboard

Redesigned a hiring process from 8 stages to 3, replacing simultaneous interview panels with an async recorded case study — solving a cross-functional scheduling bottleneck and significantly improving candidate experience and conversion.

BairesDev

Built an automated offer letter system with Excel mail merge that was adopted as team standard, eliminating manual document generation across the staffing operation.
